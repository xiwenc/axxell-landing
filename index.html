Sell more to your customers

Axxell enables webshop owners to sell more to their customer. This is achieved by recommending relevant products based on historical shopping behaviors of similar customers (Collaborative Filtering).

The major difference between Axxell and other similar web shop modules or plugins is recommended products are derived from shoppers of similar web shops. This means we are unifying shopping behavior on a global scale enabling the system to present better predictions that will help your shoppers buy more related products.

Let's illustrate what this means with a hypothetical example: Jennifer, a shopper bought a nice dress with matching shoes from Webshop J. Another shopper at Webshop M, Melissa adds the same dress to her shopping cart. Because Melissa is buying the same dress that Jennifer bought in the past elsewhere Axxell recommends the pair of shoes Jennifer bought to Melissa.
